I Wanna See A Fucking Library

I Wanna See A Fucking Library is a zine about circulation of knowledge. At the end of each semester, Alia Attar and I would send each other drafts of our final papers. In turn, we would annotate each others and respond creatively. This project is a method to create space for generative play between both intellectual and creative practices. Below is an example. A new edition is projected to come out at the end of the spring semester with other individuals involved as well.
