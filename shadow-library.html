As a library intern, I helped Samantha Chao curate Asia Art Archive’s booth for Bangkok Art Book Fair. Our concept was titled ‘Shadow Library,’ a reflection on the rejected materials of the archive, censorship and community circulations of knowledge through annotations. Part of the project included making a ‘Shadow Library’ zine that walked you through communal knowledge practices by annotating, collecting second-hand items and used material.

The zine was bound only through excess material from the archive.
