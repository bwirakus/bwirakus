I am an aspiring archivist & librarian. Professionally, I am passionate about outreach and events management for heritage non-profits, arts organisations and independent publishers.

My previous experience is a combination of marketing, librarianship and sustainable development. Social media, ephemera production and workshops are some of the responsibilities I’ve taken on in these roles. (Graphics & Print)

Eventually, I intend to do a PhD in English Literature. My academic interests include Trans Phenomenology, Sapphic and Feminist Radical Friendships, Alternative and Subaltern Epistemology and Forgetting & Memory studies. (Writing) .

Personally, I love making zines with my friends and circulating annotations and readings. It is a privilege to be receiving the education of my undergrad, so I make it a point to learn and share what I’m reading for class.
